Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Data Collection: Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.

Third-Party Services: Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Notifications: Some apps offer optional local notifications. Notification permissions are only requested when you explicitly enable this feature in Settings. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data Storage: All user preferences and app data are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any cloud service.

Contact: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at rabiou@icloud.com.
